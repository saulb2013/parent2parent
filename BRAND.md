# Parent2Parent Brand Guidelines

**For designers, illustrators, and collaborators.**

---

## Brand Overview

**Name:** Parent2Parent
**Tagline:** Previously loved. Ready for more.
**Positioning:** South Africa's trusted marketplace for quality second-hand baby and kids products.
**Mission:** Saving families money, reducing waste, and giving every item a second home.

### Brand Personality
- **Warm, not corporate** — we talk like a friend who happens to know a lot about parenting
- **Trustworthy** — parents are handing over money and trusting strangers with their kids' stuff
- **Premium but accessible** — elevated feel without being exclusive or intimidating
- **South African** — built here, for here. ZAR, local couriers, local language

### Tone of Voice
- Friendly, reassuring, concise
- "Shop pre-loved items" not "Browse our catalogue"
- "Sell in minutes" not "List your products"
- "Made for parents. Built on trust." (hero headline)
- Avoid jargon, avoid being overly cute or twee

## Logo

### Wordmark
- **"Parent"** in primary green + **"2"** in accent orange + **"Parent"** in primary green
- Set in **Playfair Display Bold** (the display font)
- Always rendered as a single line

### Icon
- Double-heart SVG in two greens (light heart behind, dark heart in front)
- The overlapping hearts represent parent-to-parent connection
- Light heart: `#52B788` (primary-light) at 70% opacity
- Dark heart: `#2D6A4F` (primary)

### Usage Rules
- Logo always appears on white or the off-white background (`#FAFAF8`)
- Minimum clear space: the width of the "2" on all sides
- Never stretch, rotate, or recolour the logo
- Do not place the logo on busy backgrounds or photos without a container

## Typography

### Fonts
| Role | Font | Weight | Source |
|------|------|--------|--------|
| Display (headings) | **Playfair Display** | 400, 500, 600, 700 | Google Fonts |
| Body (everything else) | **DM Sans** | 400, 500, 600, 700 | Google Fonts |

### Hierarchy
- **Hero headline:** Playfair Display Bold, 48–60px (responsive)
- **Section headings (h2):** Playfair Display Semibold, 24px
- **Card titles:** Playfair Display Medium, 18px
- **Body text:** DM Sans Regular, 14–16px
- **Captions/labels:** DM Sans Medium, 12–13px, muted grey
- **Buttons:** DM Sans Semibold, 14–16px

### Rules
- Headings are always Playfair Display (serif) — this gives the premium, editorial feel
- Body text is always DM Sans (sans-serif) — clean, modern, highly readable
- Never use Playfair for body text or DM Sans for major headings
- Line height: 1.5 for body, 1.2 for headings

## UI Components

### Cards
- White background (`#FFFFFF`)
- 16px border radius (`rounded-2xl`)
- 1px border in `#E8E8E4`
- Subtle shadow on hover
- Used for: listings, order summaries, form sections, info panels

### Buttons
| Type | Background | Text | Border | Use For |
|------|-----------|------|--------|---------|
| Primary | `#2D6A4F` | White | None | Main actions (Pay, Save, Submit) |
| Accent | `#F4A261` | White | None | Shopping CTAs (Shop, List Item) |
| Outline | Transparent | `#2D6A4F` | 2px `#2D6A4F` | Secondary actions (Cancel, Continue Shopping) |

- All buttons: 12px vertical padding, 24px horizontal, `rounded-lg`, semibold
- Hover: darken background + add subtle shadow
- Active: scale down to 98%
- Disabled: 50% opacity

### Inputs
- 1px border (`#E8E8E4`), `rounded-lg`
- Focus: 2px ring in `#52B788` (primary-light) with 30% opacity
- Error: border becomes `#F87171` (red-400), label turns red

### Badges / Tags
- Background: `#FFF3E0` (warm cream)
- Text: dark accent or primary colour
- Small, rounded-full, uppercase, letter-spaced

## Iconography
- Line-style icons (not filled), 2px stroke weight
- Sourced from Heroicons (outline set)
- Primary green (`#2D6A4F`) or muted grey (`#6B7280`) depending on context
- Category emojis are used in navigation and dropdowns (native platform emoji)

---

## Photography & Imagery
- Product photos are user-uploaded — we don't control these
- When creating marketing or placeholder imagery:
  - Bright, natural light
  - Clean backgrounds (white or light wood)
  - Real items in real homes — not studio-perfect catalogue shots
  - Diverse South African families
  - Warm, inviting, lived-in

---

## Layout Principles
- Max content width: `max-w-7xl` (1280px)
- Page padding: 16px mobile, 24px desktop
- Card grid: responsive — 1 col mobile, 2 col tablet, 3–4 col desktop
- Generous whitespace — the site should breathe
- Forms are single-column, max `max-w-xl` (576px)

## Key Screens (for reference)
1. **Home** — Hero with search bar, trust bar, category pills, featured listings grid
2. **Browse** — Filter sidebar + listing card grid with search
3. **Listing Detail** — Image gallery, price, condition badge, seller info, "Buy Now" CTA

5. **Profile** — Avatar, bio, listings tabs (Buying/Selling), edit form with address autocomplete
6. **Create Listing** — 3-step form: photos, item details (category, condition, age stage, parcel size), price

## Do's and Don'ts

**Do:**
- Use the green/orange palette consistently
- Keep copy short and parent-friendly
- Use Playfair for headlines to maintain the premium feel
- Use warm off-white backgrounds, never stark white pages
- Round corners generously (cards 16px, buttons 8px, avatars full circle)

**Don't:**
- Use gradients except in the hero section
- Use more than 2 accent colours in one view
- Make buttons or cards with sharp corners
- Use stock photos of models — keep it real
- Use dark mode (not designed for it yet)
- Add heavy animations — keep transitions subtle (200ms, ease)
